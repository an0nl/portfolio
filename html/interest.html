About the Movie : Flow

Flow is a visually captivating animated feature that follows a solitary black cat navigating a post-apocalyptic world ravaged by a catastrophic flood. In this silent journey, the cat encounters various animals—including a Labrador, a capybara, a lemur, and a secretarybird—each contributing to the narrative without uttering a single word. The film relies on expressive animation and sound design to convey emotions and relationships, creating a universal language that resonates with audiences of all ages.​
